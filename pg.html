5 Foods That Help Control Diabetes

Include these foods in your routine to naturally keep blood sugar levels under control.

Oats

Rich in soluble fibers (beta-glucans), oats help slow down the absorption of carbohydrates, preventing glucose spikes after meals. Click to learn more.

Beans and Legumes

Sources of plant-based protein and low-glycemic-index carbohydrates, they promote prolonged satiety and help control blood sugar levels. Click to learn more.

Dark Leafy Greens

Kale, spinach, and arugula provide fiber, magnesium, and antioxidants that help improve insulin sensitivity. Click to learn more.

Fatty Fish

Salmon, sardines, and tuna are rich in omega-3, a healthy fat that reduces inflammation and supports cardiovascular health, essential for diabetics. Click to learn more.

Berries

Strawberries, blackberries, and blueberries contain fiber and polyphenols that help control blood sugar levels and combat oxidative stress. Click to learn more.
